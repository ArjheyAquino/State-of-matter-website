Gas

A gas is defined as a state of matter consisting of particles that have neither a defined volume nor defined shape. It is one of the four fundamental states of matter, along with solids, liquids, and plasma.

Gas Example

Whether or not a substance is a gas depends on its temperature and pressure. Examples of gases at standard temperature and pressure include: air (a mixture of gases),chlorine at room temperature and pressure, ozone, oxygen, hydrogen, water vapor or steam
